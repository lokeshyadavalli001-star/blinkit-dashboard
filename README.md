# 🛒 Blinkit Business Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is a comprehensive Business Analytics Dashboard developed in Power BI using a Blinkit sales dataset. The dashboard provides insights into sales performance, customer behavior, operational efficiency, and customer satisfaction through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.

The objective of this project is to analyze sales, customer behavior, operations, and customer satisfaction to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Analyze overall business performance and revenue trends.
* Identify top-performing product categories and brands.
* Understand customer purchasing behavior.
* Monitor operational and delivery performance.
* Evaluate customer satisfaction using ratings and sentiment analysis.
* Provide actionable insights through interactive dashboards.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1️⃣ Executive Overview Dashboard

Provides a high-level view of business performance through key metrics and trends.

**Key KPIs**

* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Total Customers
* Average Order Value (AOV)
* Average Rating
* Average Delivery Time

**Visuals Included**

* Revenue by Category
* Revenue Trend Analysis
* Top Brands by Revenue
* Customer Segment Analysis
* KPI Summary Cards

### 2️⃣ Customer & Product Analytics

Focuses on customer purchasing patterns and product performance.

**Key Insights**

* Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

* Customer Segmentation
* Product Category Performance
* Revenue Contribution Analysis

**Visuals Included**

* Customer Segment Treemap
* Revenue Distribution Charts
* Product Category Analysis
* Customer Behavior Metrics
